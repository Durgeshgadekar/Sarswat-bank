Welcome to Saraswat Bank Digital Banking

At Saraswat Bank, we believe in providing our customers with modern, secure, and convenient banking services. With our digital banking solutions, you can manage your account, transfer funds, pay bills, and much more from the comfort of your home or on the go.

Our Digital Banking Services

1. Net Banking: Access your account, check balances, transfer funds, and pay bills through our secure online portal.

2. Mobile Banking: Download our mobile app to bank anytime, anywhere. Check your account details, transfer money, and enjoy other mobile-specific features.

3. UPI Payments: Make instant, seamless transactions with UPI. Send and receive money quickly with a simple and secure interface.

4. Bill Payments: Pay your utility bills, credit card bills, and more directly from your account using our digital banking options.

Why Choose Digital Banking?

Our digital banking platform is designed to make banking easier, faster, and more secure for our customers. Here's why you should choose Saraswat Bank’s digital banking:

  • 24/7 Access to your account from anywhere
  • Fast, secure, and reliable transactions
  • User-friendly interface and mobile-friendly solutions
  • Instant updates and real-time transaction details

How to Get Started?

Getting started with Saraswat Bank’s digital banking services is easy. Follow these steps:

  1. Visit our Online Registration Page to sign up for digital banking.
  2. Download the Saraswat Bank mobile app from the App Store or Google Play.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to set up your account and start banking digitally.
