Who We Are

Saraswat Bank is India's largest Urban Co-operative Bank, dedicated to providing a wide range of banking solutions to both individuals and businesses. With a rich history of more than 100 years, we have consistently evolved with the changing times to meet the needs of our customers, while maintaining our values of trust, integrity, and commitment to excellence.

Our History

Founded in 1918, Saraswat Bank has grown from a small-scale co-operative to one of the largest urban co-operative banks in India. We started with a mission to support local communities by providing reliable banking services, and today we offer a comprehensive range of products and services to over 2 million customers across the country.
